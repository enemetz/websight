Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a human right so we do not collect any user data. All processing is done on device and no data is ever collected or sold. We do not know, or want to know what websites you visit, who you email, who you call, or where you go. websight may bring you to third-party websites. The privacy practices of third-parties is governed by the third-party. websight uses Apple Maps for directions, so you are subject to Apple's privacy policy as well. websight may change its privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of this app after any change in the privacy policy will be considered acceptance of changes.
